Camp takes place one block north of the beachside town square, immediately adjacent to the main Puerto Morelos beach:

* Dance sessions take place on the ocean side of Hotel Las Arenas Puerto Morelos. We dance on the sandy ground under a retractable shade sail with a view of the ocean in the mornings and under the stars at night.
* Classes and late-night music jams take place in various spots on the hotel property, including the reception palapa, dance circle, old food truck court (big) palapa, and other spots out of the sun and wind as needed.

*Note: Starting in 2027, Alaska House, a private rental across the side street next to the hotel, continues to house staff, but is no longer available for camp use.*
